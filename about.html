ABOUT US

The Lily Flower is a boutique located in the heart of the CBD, on the Ground Floor of Willis Street’s newly renovated Grand Arcade. Shop online or visit us in store, open weekdays 8:00am - 5.30pm and Saturday 10:00am - 2:00pm for your convenience. We offer pick up, delivery or in store shopping with a range of ready-made and custom bouquets. We are known for our outstanding customer service, our creativity in floral design and the flawless quality, value and beauty of our flowers. Our flowers are sourced from the finest growers in New Zealand and around the world, providing world-class, high quality, first grade flowers – guaranteed. We have a range of flower bouquets and gifts to suit any occasion or budget available online for same day delivery throughout the Wellington Region, and offer free Wellington CBD delivery for orders over $50*. To supplement your order of beautiful flowers, we also offer a range of high quality gifts, including Living Light scented candles and diffusers, locally made chocolates, specialty gift cards and a range of indoor plants. Our Lead Florist, Anna Walraven, has over 10 years experience in both floristry and customer service in Wellington. She has spent years creating bespoke designs for a range of every day floral gifts, to small and large events and weddings, as well as corporate receptions, private homes and the hospitality industry.

Now, Anna is supported by her incredible “dream team” of talented and creative Florists, together providing a dedicated and passionate floral delivery service to the Wellington Region.
